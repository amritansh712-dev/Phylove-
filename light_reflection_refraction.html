MIRROR FORMULA & MAGNIFICATION
Relates object distance (u), image distance (v), focal length (f). Magnification m = –v/u. Negative m → real & inverted. f = R/2 always.
1/f = 1/v + 1/u
water
SNELL'S LAW OF REFRACTION
Ratio of sin of angles equals ratio of refractive indices. Light bends toward normal when entering denser medium. n = c/v where c = 3×10⁸ m/s.
n₁ sin i = n₂ sin r
lens_blur
LENS FORMULA & POWER
Convex lens f > 0, concave f < 0. Power P = 1/f(m) in Dioptres. For lenses in contact: net power is algebraic sum P = P₁ + P₂ + ...
1/v − 1/u = 1/f
science
GLASS SLAB LATERAL SHIFT
A rectangular slab produces emergent ray parallel to incident ray but displaced sideways. Lateral displacement d = t·sin(i−r)/cos(r) where t = slab thickness.
d = t·sin(i−r)/cos(r)
psychology
HUMAN EYE & DEFECTS
Near point: 25 cm. Far point: infinity (normal eye). Myopia corrected by concave lens. Hypermetropia by convex lens. Presbyopia by bifocal lenses.
Power of acc. = ciliary muscles
CONCAVE MIRROR — 6 NCERT CASES
  • Object at ∞: Real, inverted, point image at F
  • Beyond C: Real, inverted, diminished (between F & C)
  • At C: Real, inverted, same size (at C)
  • Between C & F: Real, inverted, magnified (beyond C)
  • At F: Real, image at infinity
  • Between F & P: Virtual, erect, magnified (behind mirror)
REFRACTION PHENOMENA (CH. 11)
  • Twinkling of Stars: Atmospheric refraction fluctuation
  • Advanced sunrise: ~2 min early due to atmospheric refraction
  • Blue sky: Rayleigh scattering — shorter wavelengths scatter more
  • Red sunrise/sunset: Blue scattered away, red passes through
  • Dispersion: White light → VIBGYOR through prism
  • TIR: Total Internal Reflection when angle exceeds critical angle

Complete Reference Sheet

MIRROR FORMULA
1/f = 1/v + 1/u
Spherical mirrors. R = 2f. Always use sign convention.
MIRROR MAGNIFICATION
m = −v/u = h'/h
m < 0: real, inverted. m > 0: virtual, erect.
SNELL'S LAW
n₁ sin i = n₂ sin r
Also: n = v₁/v₂ = sin i/sin r = n₂₁.
ABSOLUTE REFRACTIVE INDEX
n = c / v
c = 3×10⁸ m/s. Higher n → slower speed in medium.
LENS FORMULA
1/v − 1/u = 1/f
f > 0: convex (converging). f < 0: concave (diverging).
LENS MAGNIFICATION
m = v/u = h'/h
For lenses: m = v/u (without negative sign).
LENS POWER
P = 1/f(m)
SI unit: Dioptre (D). 1 D = 1 m⁻¹. Convex P > 0.
COMBINED LENSES
P = P₁+P₂+…
Algebraic sum for lenses in contact. Net 1/f = Σ1/fᵢ.
LATERAL DISPLACEMENT
d=t·sin(i−r)/cos r
t = slab thickness. i = incident, r = refracted angle.
POWER OF EYE
Near pt: 25 cm
Accommodation range: 25 cm (near) to ∞ (far point).
DISPERSION ORDER
V I B G Y O R
Violet bends most (shortest λ). Red bends least (longest λ).
FOCAL RELATION
f = R / 2
Radius of curvature R = 2f for spherical mirrors.
